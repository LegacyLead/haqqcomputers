Our Story

Five disciplines because clients don't arrive in one lane.

HaQQ Computers & Technology started as a single engineer fixing laptops in Ikorodu. The disciplines multiplied because the same client who needed a repair also needed a website, then a procurement plan, then a developer they'd trained themselves.

Philosophy

Depth over breadth-for-its-own-sake.

We didn't add pillars to look impressive on a homepage. Each one exists because clients kept asking for it, and we only said yes once we had someone on the team who could do it properly — not adequately, properly.

That's still the filter today: if HaQQ can't staff a discipline with someone who'd stake their name on the work, we refer you to someone who can, rather than stretch to cover it ourselves.

What We Hold To

Three working principles.

01

Show the diagnosis, not just the invoice

Whether it's a failed capacitor or a broken checkout flow, we explain what's wrong before we explain what it costs.

02

One accountable lead per project

Cross-discipline work still gets a single point of contact — you're never chasing three departments for one answer.

03

Teach it forward

The Academy exists because the same rigor we bring to client work should be transferable, not gatekept.

Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect information when you use this site or work with HaQQ.

Last updated: July 2026

On This Page

1. Information we collect 2. How we use your information 3. When we share information 4. Data retention 5. Your rights 6. Cookies and tracking 7. Security 8. Children's privacy 9. Changes to this policy 10. Contact us

1. Information we collect

We collect information you give us directly, and nothing more than that. This includes:

  • Contact form submissions: your name, email address, phone number, and the content of your message.
  • WhatsApp conversations: your phone number and message content, handled through WhatsApp directly — subject to WhatsApp's own privacy policy for transmission.
  • Academy enrollment: name, contact details, and any information you provide when enquiring about or enrolling in a course.
  • Service or repair intake: device details and contact information needed to carry out a repair, consultation, or delivery.

We do not currently use analytics or advertising cookies on this site. If that changes, this policy will be updated first.

2. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to enquiries and quote requests
  • Deliver the service, repair, product, or Academy enrollment you've requested
  • Send you updates directly related to a job or order you've placed with us
  • Keep basic business records, including for tax and warranty purposes

We do not use your information for purposes you haven't agreed to, and we don't send marketing messages unless you've opted in.

3. When we share information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share it when:

  • It's needed to deliver a service you've requested (for example, a courier for device delivery)
  • We're required to by law or a valid legal request
  • You've explicitly asked us to (for example, referring you to a partner for a service we don't offer)

4. Data retention

We keep contact and transaction records for as long as needed to fulfil the service and meet our legal and tax obligations, typically no longer than necessary for those purposes. You can ask us to delete your information at any time, subject to records we're legally required to keep.

5. Your rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act (2023), you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Withdraw consent for any optional communication at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details at the bottom of this page.

6. Cookies and tracking

This site does not currently set tracking or advertising cookies. It may use strictly necessary technical storage (for example, to remember a form's state while you're filling it in). If we add analytics in future, we'll disclose it here and provide a way to opt out.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share with us, including limiting who on our team can access it. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security, but we don't treat that as an excuse to be careless with your data.

8. Children's privacy

Our Tech Academy accepts students aged 13 and up. Where a student is a minor, we communicate primarily with the enrolling parent or guardian and only collect the information necessary to run the course.

9. Changes to this policy

If we materially change how we handle your data, we'll update this page and revise the "last updated" date above. Significant changes will be communicated directly to affected clients where possible.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to haqqcomputers@gmail.com or via WhatsApp at 0707 616 3345.
